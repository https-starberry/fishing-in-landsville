Fishing in Landsville

Game Synopsis

[Fishing in Landsville] is a game focused on overfishing. Overfishing is a huge problem that most people don't think about when discussing climate change and global warming.

Our game, [Fishing in Landsville] is a game about fishing in a smart and responsible way, keeping people who rely on fishing healthy, while preventing the fish from dying out.

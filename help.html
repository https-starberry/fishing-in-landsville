Help / GamePlay

Game Objective

Manage the amount of fish you need to catch to feed the villagers while making sure you don't catch too much fish that it destroys the balance of the ocean's food web. Make it to the end of day 11 by keeping everyone fed, while maintaining the fish population.

Controls + How to Play

Use the WASD keys to move around the map. Walk into doors and buttons to interact with them.
